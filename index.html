Graphic designing - The career graph!

As it covers many industries around the world, the graphic designing scope in India is extreme. No space goes without their help. The following are the must consider areas for designing:

Magazines and newspapers:

The print media is not all about the textual content inside it. It also comprises graphics that help the readers get the message.

Advertising and marketing:

The world of advertisements is dry without creativity. Not everyone wishes to use models for publicity. A designer can create the best of all and make ads look alluring.

Brand management:

The icon and logo are something that the brand is all about. Graphic designers are in charge of imprinting the label of a firm into the hearts and minds with the best design.

Electronic media:

The world today focuses everything on gadgets for every little need. Digital marketing, advertising, and attention-seeking are just another job sector for a designer.

Web designing:

Websites are a collection of pages that carry specific information. Regardless of its rich content, it is empty if the graphics are not up to the mark.

What can you expect in job roles?

The field of graphic designing scope in India comprises several job roles that you may love to handle. It includes art manager, logo designer, web designer, animator, creative director, illustration artist, and many more. The job profile you get depends upon the qualification you carry, your caliber, and your experience. You can also shift your focus to an industrial designer, architectural and engineering drafter, design consultant, etc. if you wish to take an uncommon yet beautiful role. The specifications for the profiles are increasing with time, and job roles are getting specific. Once you set your foot inside your expectations and grow your resume, you can even choose to pick companies abroad and live the life of your dreams!

Graphic designing role - what does it pay?

The pay scale for an individual varies depending upon several factors. It includes the following:

Stage

The step of your career has a lot to do with the amount you get in hand. You will always receive a handful of amounts in the beginning. As you grow, the pay increases with your designation.

Industry

The industry that you decide to work and design for says a lot about your earnings. The higher the industry value and need, the higher is the pay in the pocket.Â 

Company

The firm that you choose also determines the pay you receive. Working for a local brand at the same post and an international brand can change your package vividly.

Caliber

The initial package for every designer varies. It is because every designer comes with a different set of skills. The higher and trendy the skills, the better are the changes for high payment.

Starting the career as a newbie, you can expect the package to be around 1.5-2 LPA. As you grow, you will encounter a mid-experience level with an average of 3-5 LPA. Once you gain ample experience, you can receive up to 7 LPA and above, depending on your work. The figures also vary in different stages as per the company, location, and the role you pick!
